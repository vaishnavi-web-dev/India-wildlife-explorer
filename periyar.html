Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary

Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary is located in Kerala and is famous for its beautiful forests, lakes and elephant reserve.

The sanctuary is one of India's most popular eco-tourism destinations and is known for boat safaris and biodiversity.

Famous Animals

  • Asian Elephant
  • Bengal Tiger
  • Indian Bison
  • Sambar Deer

Best Time To Visit

October to February

Location

Kerala, India
