Kaziranga National Park

Kaziranga National Park is located in Assam and is one of the most famous wildlife sanctuaries in India.

It is globally known for the One-Horned Rhinoceros and is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Famous Animals

  • One-Horned Rhinoceros
  • Bengal Tiger
  • Wild Buffalo
  • Asian Elephant

Best Time To Visit

November to April

Location

Assam, India
