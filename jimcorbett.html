Jim Corbett National Park

Jim Corbett National Park is located in Uttarakhand and is India's oldest national park.

It is famous for Bengal Tigers, wildlife safaris and beautiful forests.

Famous Animals

  • Bengal Tiger
  • Asian Elephant
  • Leopard
  • Deer

Best Time To Visit

November to June

Location

Uttarakhand, India
