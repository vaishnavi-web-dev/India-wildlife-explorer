Gir National Park

Gir National Park is located in Gujarat and is the only natural habitat of Asiatic Lions in the world.

The park is famous for wildlife safaris, dry forests and rich biodiversity.

Famous Animals

  • Asiatic Lion
  • Leopard
  • Striped Hyena
  • Deer

Best Time To Visit

December to March

Location

Gujarat, India
