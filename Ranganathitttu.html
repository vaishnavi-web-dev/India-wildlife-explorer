Ranganathittu Bird Sanctuary

Ranganathittu Bird Sanctuary is located in Karnataka near Mysore and is the largest bird sanctuary in the state.

It is famous for migratory birds, river islands and beautiful boating experiences.

Famous Birds

  • Painted Stork
  • Pelican
  • Heron
  • Kingfisher

Best Time To Visit

November to June

Location

Karnataka, India
